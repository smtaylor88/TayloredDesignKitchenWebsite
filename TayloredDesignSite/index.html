My Experience

I have been designing kitchens and helping my clients select the right cabinets for over 20 years. I learned the business while working for Hugh M Woods (21 years). If you remember them, it's been awhile. Afterward I worked for Jordan Building Supply as kitchen designer (6 years) Finally, I started my own business in 2007 and now have a long list of very pleased clients and contractors. There are pictures of many of my projects on this website.

Why Choose Me?

I work with many of the areas finest custom home builders and they all know the quality of my work and my precision standards. Contractors, as well as, many of my previous clients, are quick to refer me when there is a kitchen or bath project that needs cabinets. Quality and on-time project completion are my top priorities, once the style selection and room design are complete. The majority of my projects come from referrals and I take pride in seeing each project through to the end.

Satisfaction Guaranteed!

I really don't like the word Satisfaction. To me, it means that the job was done okay, maybe a C+ on the report card.

I prefer it when my customers are delighted and excited to see their newly completed room. It should inspire them and make them very happy and I try to deliver on that every time!
